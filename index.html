Festival

Día de los Muertos Festival is a unique festival featuring a full day Dia los Muertos cultural experience with live music, performances, art, kid's zone, food and drink. The festival brings together a dynamic blend of music with vibrant Día de los Muertos traditions.

Proceeds from the festival will benefit Easter Seals Central Texas’ mission of promoting independence and creating opportunities for people with disabilities.


Be sure to bring your photo I.D. for entry into the festival. Cash only will be accepted for ticket purchases day of the event.
